# Stockbridge Coffee - Packaging Text Examples

These packaging text options are based on the brand values and messaging from the Stockbridge Coffee website.

---

## Option 1: Story-Focused

**FROM FARM TO CUP, WITH LOVE**

At Stockbridge Coffee, great coffee starts with great relationships. We work directly with farmers across Latin America, supporting fair trade and sustainable practices at every step.

Roasted by hand in small batches in Stockbridge, Edinburgh, each bean is carefully crafted to highlight the unique flavours and character of its origin.

For us, coffee is more than a drink—it's about connecting communities, celebrating craftsmanship, and sharing the warmth behind every cup.

**100% Sustainable | Single Origin | Artisan Roasted**

---

## Option 2: Values-Driven

**CRAFTED WITH PASSION, ROASTED WITH CARE**

We believe in doing one thing extraordinarily well. Sourcing from 15+ countries and serving 50,000+ happy customers, our commitment remains the same: quality, sustainability, and community.

Every batch is roasted by experienced artisans who bring decades of passion to their craft, ensuring each cup delivers the distinctive notes and aromas that make specialty coffee exceptional.

Because coffee is more than a morning ritual—it's a celebration of the people, places, and stories behind every bean.

**Fair Trade | Small Batch | Edinburgh Roasted**

---

## Option 3: Product-Focused

**SMALL BATCH. BIG FLAVOUR.**

This coffee represents everything we believe in: direct relationships with farmers, sustainable sourcing, and artisan roasting that honours the bean's natural character.

Hand-roasted in Stockbridge, Edinburgh, our single-origin beans are selected for their exceptional quality and roasted to perfection to bring you notes of floral, citrus, honey, and tea-like complexity.

From Latin American farms to your cup—transparency, quality, and care in every step.

**Altitude: 1,800-2,000m | Light-Medium Roast | 100% Sustainable**

---

## Option 4: Community-Focused (Shortest/Most Concise)

**CONNECTING COMMUNITIES, ONE CUP AT A TIME**

Sourced directly from Latin American farmers who share our commitment to quality and sustainability. Roasted by hand in small batches in Stockbridge, Edinburgh.

Great coffee starts with great relationships—from farm to roaster to you.

**Single Origin | Fair Trade | Artisan Roasted in Edinburgh**

**Stockbridge Coffee | Est. Edinburgh**

---

## Key Brand Elements

Each option emphasizes:
- **Sustainability**: 100% sustainable sourcing, fair trade practices
- **Craftsmanship**: Small-batch, artisan roasting in Edinburgh
- **Community**: Direct relationships with farmers, connecting people
- **Quality**: Single-origin beans, careful selection and roasting
- **Origin**: Latin American sourcing, Edinburgh roasting

## Usage Notes

- **Option 1** works best for larger packaging with more space for storytelling
- **Option 2** emphasizes the brand's commitment and statistics
- **Option 3** is ideal for product-specific packaging with tasting notes
- **Option 4** is perfect for minimal packaging or smaller bag sizes
